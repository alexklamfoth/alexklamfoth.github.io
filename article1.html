One MusicFest has become one of the largest Urban Music festivals in the Southeast. This festival has hosted the likes of 2 Chainz, Outkast, Usher, and more. The festival will be hosted on October 28 & 29th of 2023. The following lineup has been annoucned for the festival.

Lineup for One MusicFest 2023




One MusicFest was started all the way back in 2010, and has been hosted in Atlanta every year since. They are expected to host over 45,000 people.
